About Front Row Harford!

Front Row Harford! was created by Kevin Bradley, who believes every performance deserves a packed house and a standing ovation. A Harford County resident for more than 40 years, Kevin loves this community ‚Äî especially its vibrant student and local theater scene.

Kevin has been producing and promoting live events for decades. He produced a Toyota concert series featuring Grammy-nominated singer/songwriter Laura Branigan and Little River Band, and together with his wife Marilyn and their family, created Harford County‚Äôs largest outdoor Christmas festival, Celebrate the Light in Joppa, MD, welcoming tens of thousands of guests.

Beyond Harford County, Kevin served as Chairman of the Board for the Roanoke Island Historical Association, which produces The Lost Colony, the longest-running outdoor symphonic drama in the United States. Before becoming chairman, he led the marketing committee, where his primary responsibility was ticket sales. During his tenure, both ticket sales and ticket revenue grew substantially.

Front Row Harford! is Kevin‚Äôs gift to the community. Listings are free for schools and local theater companies, and the mission is simple: remove the barriers that make it hard for residents to discover and attend live performances. Through a clear, easy-to-use online calendar and an active social media campaign, Front Row Harford! helps neighbors find shows, buy tickets, and fill the seats for the students and performers who have worked so hard to be on stage.
