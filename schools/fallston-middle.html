About the show:
Xanadu JR. is a G-rated musical adaptation of the 1980s cult classic film, featuring a 1980s pop-rock score by Jeff Lynne and John Farrar. The story follows Kira, a Greek muse who descends to Venice Beach, California, to inspire a struggling artist, Sonny, to create the first roller disco.
