Between the Lines

Director: Jennifer English

Venue: The Amoss Center, 200 Thomas Run Rd, Bel Air, MD

School Contact: (410) 638-3804

Dates & Times: Fri Jan 9 @ 7PM • Sat Jan 10 @ 2PM & 7PM

Tickets: Adults $12 • Seniors & Students $10

Recommended Audience: PG-13 (Teens & up)

About the show:
Between the Lines is a powerful, uplifting musical about a young girl who feels unseen in a noisy, demanding world and turns to fairytales for refuge. When the characters within the pages come alive, they challenge her to find her voice, claim her place, and recognize her worth. Blending imagination with real-world courage, Between the Lines reminds us that while stories can inspire us, the most important story to be told is our own and everyone deserves to be seen, heard, and valued.
