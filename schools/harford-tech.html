Mamma Mia!

Director: Jennifer English

Venue: The Amoss Center, 200 Thomas Run Rd, Bel Air, MD

School Contact: (410) 638-3804

Dates & Times: Fri April 17 @ 7PM • Sat Apr 18 @ 2PM & 7PM

Tickets: Adults $12 • Seniors & Students $10

Recommended Audience: Family

About the show:
Get ready to dance, jive, and have the time of your life! Mamma Mia! – the beloved stage musical that inspired the hit film – brings ABBA’s greatest songs to life in a story full of love, laughter, and friendship. Set on a sun-drenched Greek island, this irresistible tale follows a young woman’s search for her birth father on the eve of her wedding—leading to joyful chaos, unexpected reunions, and a celebration you’ll never forget.
