About the show:
Between the Lines is an empowering musical based on the book by Jodi Picoult and Samantha van Leer, focusing on 17-year-old Delilah, an outcast struggling with her parents' divorce and a new school. She escapes into a favorite fairy-tale book, developing a romance with its protagonist, Prince Oliver. As the lines between fiction and reality blur, they must navigate their separate worlds to find a way to be together.
