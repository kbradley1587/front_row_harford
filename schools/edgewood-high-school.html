About the show:
You've heard the way that Hans told the story. Come and see what REALLY happened! This Golden Age retelling of The Princess and the Pea, featuring music by Mary Rogers, pits our heroine Winnifred against the world’s most legendary boy-mom, Queen Agravain. Heralds, hijinks, and hilarity are sure to keep you entertained as the Edgewood Theatre Group presents this classic family musical.
